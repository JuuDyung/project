HCI project

Scenario

The main character, white-girl, runs an errand to her grandmother's house.

On the rough road, she encounters various obstacles along the way.

There's a wolf in the grandmother's house and white-girl's cloak stained with wolf's blood.

Demo

Development Environment

  • Kinect
  • C#
  • Unity

How to play

Explanations for each obstacles

  • tree : Use your hands to tip over the top of the tree with your hands and move forward.
  • wolf : Wolves move around the map in a certain pattern. Use your hands to drop them on the cliff.
  • trap : Move away from the trap or lure the wolf into the trap.
  • cannibal plant : Use a rope to cross over the cannibal plant.
  • rope : If you hold both hands, you can hold the rope and if you open both hands, you can release the rope.
  • chandelier : Grab the chandelier and cross the obstacles.
  • arrows : Block the falling arrow with your hand, or move in time.
  • ladder : Use the box to narrow down the distance from the ladder.
